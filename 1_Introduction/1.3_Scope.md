This project looks for achieve these items:
* **Open and accessible:** The project is open-source, making it accessible and affordable for anyone interested in wearable electronics.
* **Scalable manufacturing:** It has been designed to be manufactured in different formats, ranging from a DIY kit to a semi-industrial product, either partially assembled or ready to use.
* **Suitable for all skill levels:** The product can be used by a wide range of users, from children and hobbyists to students, educators, makers, and professionals.
