The proposed solution should allow wearable electronic circuits to be tested using wearable electronic components while ensuring reliable electrical connections.

It should provide a fast, easy, and convenient way to prototype and validate circuits before sewing them into a textile project.

Overall, the solution should:

Provide reliable and robust electrical connections without the need for sewing.
Offer an alternative to crocodile clips and temporary wired connections.
Follow a concept similar to a protoboard, allowing components to be quickly connected, tested, modified, and reused.
Simplify troubleshooting by reducing connection-related issues during the prototyping stage.
Speed up the development process of wearable electronic projects.
